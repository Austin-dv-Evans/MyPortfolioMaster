Get to know me

Family Comes First

My wife and my daughter are the most important things in my life. My wife Renee is from upstate New York, she loves the Grateful Dead and is the best mom in the world. Our daughter Luna, was born in September of 2020, she is absolutely perfect. We have a 6 year old American Bull Hound named Jerry. He is a very good boy. We live in my beautiful home town of Denver, Colorado.

Doing What I Love

Software Engineering has been such a blessing to me. I love developing apps and websites. It is something that I will be doing the rest of my life. Changing my career was the best decision I have ever made. I used to be your favorite local bartender. I am still your favorite funky local DJ. I have always been passionate about creating experiences. Now I create experiences with code. Coding is my passion. With the skills I am developing, some teamwork and our amazing community, we can create anything. For this dev, the sky's the limit.
